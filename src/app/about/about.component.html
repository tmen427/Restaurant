Yummy is the first solo project of Chef Walter White. Inspired by Walter’s upbringing in downtown New York and his experience leading the city’s most celebrated kitchens, Yummy is a neighborhood seasonal restaurant elevated by fine dining training and technique. Located on the in Adam Street, NY, the dining room and bar occupy a series of rooms with floor to 16-foot-ceiling windows leading to an open kitchen. The food at Yumy is rooted in asian cuisine but draws inspiration from around the world, primarily the flavors from our childhoods, traveling, and the diverse cuisine in New York City.
